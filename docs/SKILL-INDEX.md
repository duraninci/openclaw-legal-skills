## Contract Review & Negotiation

### contract-review
**File**: `skills/contract-review/SKILL.md`

General-purpose contract analysis skill. Reviews any contract type and identifies issues by severity. Includes frameworks for:
- Issue spotting and classification
- Risk assessment
- Suggested modifications
- Negotiation considerations

**Best for**: NDAs, service agreements, vendor contracts, general commercial agreements

**Includes examples**: NDA review, SaaS agreement, M&A agreement, balanced agreement

## Corporate & Partnership

### llc-operating-agreement
**File**: `skills/llc-operating-agreement/SKILL.md`

Deep analysis of LLC and partnership operating agreements. Position-aware review that adapts based on whether you're majority member, minority member, or incoming partner.

**Covers**:
- Capital structure and contributions
- Distribution waterfalls and preferences
- Governance and voting thresholds
- Buy-sell provisions and triggers
- Exit rights (tag-along, drag-along, ROFR)
- Management and control provisions

**Best for**: Reviewing operating agreements, negotiating co-founder deals, evaluating minority investments

- Advisor equity (FAST agreements)

**Best for**: Co-founder agreements, early-stage equity structures, advisor grants
